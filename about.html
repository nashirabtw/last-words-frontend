About Last Words

Last Words is a quiet place on the internet.

It was created for the words people carry with them — the ones that didn’t fit into conversations, timelines, or social feeds.

This is not social media. There are no likes, no comments, no profiles.

Each message lives on a real place on Earth, marked only by a single pin. Once placed, it becomes part of the map — visible, silent, and still.

Some people leave memories. Some leave apologies. Some leave things they never said out loud.

Last Words exists to give those words a place.
