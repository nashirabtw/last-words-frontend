Frequently Asked Questions

What is Last Words?

Last Words is a global map where people leave messages tied to a specific place on Earth.

Is it anonymous?

Yes. You may leave your name, but it is optional. No accounts are required.

Why does it cost money to publish a message?

The fee helps keep the platform online, moderated, and free from spam. It also ensures that messages are placed intentionally.

Can I delete a message?

At the moment, messages are meant to be permanent. This reinforces the idea of leaving something behind with intention.

How long does a message stay on the map?

Messages are intended to remain indefinitely, as long as the platform exists.

Is the content moderated?

Yes. Messages that contain hate, abuse, or illegal content may be removed.

Can I use Last Words for memorials?

Yes. Many people use it to leave words for loved ones, places, or moments that mattered.
